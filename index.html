About Our Church

A welcoming church family committed to worship, prayer, sound teaching, and raising disciples for the Kingdom of God.

Background

Christ the King Christian Center began on January 03, 1999 with 3 families as God laid the vision on Apostle Chibuike Azuoru and his wife, Pastor Mary Azuoru. Following Apostle Chibuike’s ordination by Pastor J.D. Brown of Faith Chapel Church of God, they established a worship fellowship where believers could grow, serve, and develop their spiritual gifts. Today, what began with only 3 families has grown into an International Christian Worship Church drawing people from all walks of life and nations.

Apostle Chibuike Azuoru and Pastor Mary Azuoru
Founders of Christ the King Christian Center

Our Mission

To glorify Jesus Christ through worship, prayer, the Word, and loving community—equipping believers to be overcomers who impact others for the Kingdom of God.

Our Vision

A vibrant, multi-ethnic, multi-generational church where the truth sets people free, lives are transformed, and the nations rejoice in Christ the King.
