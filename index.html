Our Story

About Our Church

A welcoming family committed to worship, prayer, sound teaching,
and raising disciples for the Kingdom of God.

Founder

Apostle Chibuike Azuoru

Christ the King Christian Center was founded on January 3, 1999, when God laid the vision on Apostle Chibuike Azuoru — beginning with just three faithful families who answered the call.

Following his ordination by Pastor J.D. Brown of Faith Chapel Church of God, Apostle Azuoru established a worship fellowship where believers could grow, serve, and develop their spiritual gifts. Under his apostolic leadership, what began with three families has grown into a thriving International Christian Worship Church drawing people from all walks of life and every nation.
