Our Story

About Our Church

A welcoming family committed to worship, prayer, sound teaching,
and raising disciples for the Kingdom of God.

Founders

Apostle & Pastor Azuoru

Christ the King Christian Center was founded on January 3, 1999, with just three families when God laid the vision on Apostle Chibuike Azuoru and his wife, Pastor Mary Azuoru.

Following Apostle Chibuike's ordination by Pastor J.D. Brown of Faith Chapel Church of God, they established a worship fellowship where believers could grow, serve, and develop their spiritual gifts. What began in faith with three families has grown into an International Christian Worship Church drawing people from all walks of life and every nation.
